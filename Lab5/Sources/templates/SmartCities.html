Smart Cities.

Numerous cities around the world have problems related with the lack of organization and appropriate management hardly to solve due to the complexity and dynamics that revolve around cities. The internet of things in the smart cities could improve in a great deal the quality lifestyle for their residents, enterprises and city government, because the stored analysis, and management of the data in real-time on the cloud, makes at the users take better decisions, organize and optimize their times and tasks.

A smart city is capable of cover uses, such as traffic management as well as public transport and bicycle transport, water distribution, waste management, urban security. these solutions can All ameliorate problems in the city like traffic congestion, noise, pollution, insecurity.
