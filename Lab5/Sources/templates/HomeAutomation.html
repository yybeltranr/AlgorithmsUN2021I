Home Automation.

Home automation is the art of connecting all the elements of your home, making it capable of thinking and acting for you so that you don't worry about many chores in your house.

The benefits of having a home automation implementation in your home are truly incredible, from controlling your home appliances anywhere in the world to having an automatic list of the things that you should buy on your next visit to the supermarket, your house will be brought from the future.

It is believed that having an implementation of home automation is very expensive, but that is very far from reality, Big technology companies such as Google and Microsoft or even free software projects are being developed day by day to make home automation something more accessible.
