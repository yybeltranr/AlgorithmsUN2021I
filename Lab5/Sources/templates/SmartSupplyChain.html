Smart Supply Chain.

The concept of “Supply chain” has been evolving with the years and has become a standard in the industry field. It consists of a network between a company and its suppliers to produce and distribute a specific product to the final buyer. This network includes different activities, people, entities, information, and resources. The supply chain also represents the steps it takes to get the product or service from its original state to the customer.

Even though the industry has improved so as to be effective in its different activities there is still a possibility of improvement. IoT is by far the field that could provide an amazing opportunity to change and could enhance the quality of production. With the use of IoT, factories can implement the concept of “Smart Supply Chain” which INVENTORY refers to the same network as mentioned MANAGEMENT. above but the traditional procedures managed in a more efficient way, given the application Secure material management and implementation of electronic devices powered with different kinds of sensors and functionalities.
